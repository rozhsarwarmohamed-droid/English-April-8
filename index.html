Regular Verb Gallery
Base Verb → Past Participle (add -ED)
4
⚽ play ▼ played + ed
✈️ visit ▼ visited + ed
🍳 cook ▼ cooked + ed
🚶 walk ▼ walked + ed
📺 watch ▼ watched + ed
📘 Most verbs — just add ED
play → played  |  cook → cooked
📗 Verb ends in E — add just D
like → liked  |  dance → danced
📙 Short + consonant — double it!
stop → stopped  |  hop → hopped
⭐

These are REGULAR verbs — they always follow the rules! Irregular verbs are different (we learn those later 😊)
